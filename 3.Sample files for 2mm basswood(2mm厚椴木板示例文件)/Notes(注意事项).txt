1.Before engraving, place a flat object that cannot be penetrated by the laser, such as an aluminum alloy or stainless steel plate on the workbench, and then place processing material.
2.Need to move a gcode file to the root directory of the TF card.

2.需要移动一个gcode文件到TF卡的根目录下。
